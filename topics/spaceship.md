## Buying a Domain

### Description

This details how to purchase a domain name on Spaceship.

### References

- [How to use Domain Management](https://kb.porkbun.com/article/173-how-to-use-domain-management)

### Steps

1. Visit the [Register a domain](https://www.spaceship.com/domains) page on a web browser. Log into your Spaceship account if you have not already.

2. Use the provided **Search for your domain name** search bar to look for a domain name.

3. From the list of available domains, add a domain name of your choice to your cart by clicking its corresponding **Add to cart** button. Click the **Cart** button on the top right once it has been added.

4. In your cart, click the **Customize in cart** link to get to the **Shopping cart** page.

5. In the **Shopping cart** page, expand the domain's corresponding **Registration** dropdown and select the duration of your domain registration (i.e. `1 Year`). Click the **Checkout** button.

6. In the **Order details** page, choose your payment method, and click the **Pay Now** button to continue with your purchase.

## Update Authoritative Nameserver

### Description

This details how to update the authoritative nameserver for a domain.

### References

- [How to Change Nameservers](https://kb.porkbun.com/article/22-how-to-change-nameservers)

### Steps

1. Visit the [Domain Manager](https://www.spaceship.com/application/domain-list-application) page on a web browser. Log into your Spaceship account if you have not already.

2. From the list of available domains in your account, click the domain you wish to update.

3. Click the domain's corresponding **Nameservers & DNS** menu item.

4. In the **Nameservers** section, click the corresponding **Change** button.

5. In the **Update nameservers?** form, select the **Custom nameservers** option, and add any intended nameservers to the text area, each on a new line.

6. Click the **Save nameserver settings** button to save the changes.
